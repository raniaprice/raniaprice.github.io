Introduction

Retrograde

Retrograde is a digital magazine dedicated to the revival of the Y2K aesthetic, spotlighting the iconic trends of the late 90s and early 2000s that are making a comeback. From butterfly clips and low-rise jeans to rhinestone accessories and velour tracksuits, we explore how these nostalgic styles are being reinterpreted by mainly Gen Z and Millennials today. With a mix of trend reports, style guides, and pop-culture deep-dives, Retrograde bridges the gap between past and present, proving that what was once “out” is officially back “in.”
